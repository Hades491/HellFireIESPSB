About Voice for Peace

Our initiative nurtures harmony, education, and collaboration among students. We believe peace begins with understanding, learning, and mutual respect. It also refers to various initiatives advocating peaceful conflict resolution, such as the Voice of Peace radio station founded by Abie Nathan to foster dialogue between Israelis and Palestinians.

Our Mission

Education

Empowering students with knowledge and skills for peacebuilding, conflict resolution, and social justice.

Advocacy

Amplifying voices of peace worldwide through campaigns and social media.

Educational Programs

We organize seminars, guest lectures, and interactive sessions to empower students with the knowledge and skills needed to promote peace and social justice.
